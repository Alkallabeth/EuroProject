How to Play

Setup

At the start of the game, players are divided into 2 teams: the Girondins and the Mountains. Each player represents a historical figure from that political party.

Objective

The goal of the game is to get your team to the finish line first and to have the most popularity points.

Playing the Game

Each turn, the player will roll a dice and advance the number of spaces shown on the dice. Each space has the following results:

Gain 1 Popularity Point
Lose 1 Popularity Point
Complete a task. If you pass, gain popularity. If you fail, lose popularity.
Award popularity point(s) to a random player
An event space. If the event is incomplete, players MUST stop on the space and successfully complete 3 tasks to complete the event for everyone. The first player to successfully complete the event gains popularity points. If a player fails the tasks, they will move back 3 spaces.

The Liberal Phase

During the first part of the game, all players will work together to complete the events of the Liberal Phase.

The Radical Phase

During the second part, each team will work to stop the other team from reaching the finish.

The End

When one player reaches the finish line, that player's team wins! Whichever player on the winning team has the most popularity points is the overall winner.
