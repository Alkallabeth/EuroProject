How to Play

Setup

At the start of the game, players are divided into 2 teams: the Girondins and the Mountains. These teams will work together to hinder the other team.

Objective

The goal of the game is to get to the finish first and to have the most Popularity Points.


Playing the Game

Each turn, the player will roll a dice and advance the number of spaces shown on the dice. Each space has the following results:

Gain 1 Popularity Point.
Lose 1 Popularity Point. You can't have negative popularity.
Complete a task. If you pass, gain popularity. If you fail, lose popularity.
Award a popularity point to a random player.
An event space. If the event is incomplete, players MUST stop on the space and successfully complete 3 tasks to complete the event for everyone. The first player to successfully complete the event gains popularity points. If a player fails the tasks, they will move back 3 spaces and lose popularity.

The Liberal Phase

During the first part of the game, all players will work to complete the events of the Liberal Phase and to gain popularity.

The Radical Phase

During the second part, each team will work to stop the other team from reaching the finish by completing tasks. Events in this phase result in the furthest player on the other team moving back 3 spaces. However, if any player has less than 5 popularity once one player has reached this phase, they will be guillotined and will be out of the game.

The End

When one player reaches the finish line, that player's team wins! Whichever player on the winning team has the highest score is the overall winner. Score is calculated by subtracting the player's distance from the finish from the player's popularity (popularity - distance).
